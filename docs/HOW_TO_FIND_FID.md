# 📖 How to Find Your FID — WhiteOut Survival

Your **FID (Fighter ID)** is your unique player identifier in WhiteOut Survival. You need it to register with the bot and start receiving automatic gift code redemptions.

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Open WhiteOut Survival
Launch the WhiteOut Survival game on your device.

### Step 2: Tap Your Avatar
Tap on your **player avatar** in the **top-left corner** of the main screen.

### Step 3: Find Your FID
Your **FID** is displayed directly below your nickname on the player info screen. It's a number like `123456789`.

## Register with the Bot

Once you have your FID, register with the bot using:

```
/register 123456789
```

Replace `123456789` with your actual FID.

The bot will:
1. Look up your player data from the game API
2. Confirm your nickname and furnace level
3. Register you in your alliance's member list
4. Start redeeming gift codes for you automatically

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my FID the same as my username?
No. Your FID is a **numeric identifier** unique to your account. Your username/nickname can be changed, but your FID stays the same forever.

### Can someone hack my account with my FID?
No. Your FID is a **public identifier** — similar to a player tag in other games. It cannot be used to log into your account or access any private information.

### My FID doesn't work with the bot?
Make sure you're copying the **correct number** (not your state ID or alliance ID). The FID is displayed on your player profile when you tap your avatar.
